Grilled Cheese recipe

These are the following ingredients you will need for the perfect grilled cheese sandwich!

  • Bread, any kind is fine.
  • Cheese, again any kind is fine
  • Butter to get a nice crisp on the grilled cheeses
  • Some black pepper to grind over the melted cheeseh when done.

Here are the cooking instructions!

  1. Melt the butter in the pan
  2. Once the butter is melted, add your sandwich and wait till it is golden brown making sure the pan doesnt get to hot
  3. Flip once golden brown
  4. Once both sides are crispy and the cheese is melted you can take them out of the pan.

Grilled cheeses go well with

  • Mayonaise,
  • Peri Peri sauce
  • And many many more sauces.
